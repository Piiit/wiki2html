== MID-TERM Exam -- 11.Nov.2013 -- 10:30-12:30 ==

Topics of the Mid-Term exam are: Formal Language Theory, Notions of Grammar and Derivation, Context-Free-Languages and Context-Free-Grammars, Normal Forms for CFLs, Regular Languages and Regular Expressions, Determinist nad Non-deterministic Finite Automata, Parse Trees and Ambiguous Grammars.

Students who pass the mid-term exam can avoid the part of the final exam concerning Formal Languages.
